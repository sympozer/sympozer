Turn any form into a wizard simply by adding a data-wizard attribute and passing it an options object.

<form data-wizard"" id="basicwizard">
	<fieldset title="Step 1">
		<legend>No Validation</legend>
		...
	</fieldset>
	<fieldset title="Step 2">
		<legend>Just Text</legend>
		...
	</fieldset>
</form>

The wizard has jQuery Validation plugin support built-in. All you need to do is turn on the validate: true option on and you instantly have a wizard with form validation!

<form action="#" data-wizard="{finishButton: true, titleClick: true, block: true, validate: true}" class="form-horizontal">
...
</form>
